ABOUT ME




Wendy is presently working with WellSpring Coaching and Counseling Center where she is the Co-Director of Coaching. Wendy received her MA from Wheaton Graduate School in Educational Ministries, with an emphasises on Interpersonal Communication and Counseling. While there, she was an intern in Career Development. She also completed Postgraduate Studies from Ball State University in Advanced Counseling and Counseling Practicum. She also holds certificates in: Prepare and Enrich premarital counseling; Premarital Interpersonal Choices and Knowledge (P.I.C.K.); Redemptive Marital Therapy I and II: Professional Life Coaching Wendy”s practical experience comes from over 33 years of marriage, raising four children and having an active role working with individuals and groups in the academic environments, churches and marketplace.

My SERVICES



Coaching

Coaching is about moving towards results. My work is helping you to explore, find tools and resources, and empower you to MOVE in the direction toward the goal(s) desirous of attaining, especially with key relationships: Marital, family, work and friendships.

Group

Offered:
How To Avoid Marrying a Jerk or Jerkette(Premarital Interpersonal Choices and Knowledge)
Boundaries Choosing essential “guardrails” for life and living / When to say Yes, How to say No to take control of your life.
DivorceCare Find help, discover hope, and experience healing from separation or divorce.

Marital

The Marriage “engine” needs tuning up, renewed parts, and lots of work to continue to run smoothly. Gain new perspectives, acquire tools, appreciation and respect for each other by moving in directions of better communication and understanding.

My goal is to provide a relaxed and accepting environment for you to explore where you are, and where you want to be; offering tools, insight, and helping you to know that YOU CAN, with help, achieve a better quality of life. Faith based (non-discriminatory), professional, affordable and accessible services for you.
